Hi Little Scientist!

What you need:

  • Three transparent cups
  • Red, blue, and yellow food coloring
  • Water
  • Small stirrers or spoons

How to do:

  1. Step 1: Fill each cup with water.
  2. Step 2: Add a few drops of red food coloring to the first cup, blue to the second cup, and yellow to the third cup.
  3. Step 3: Stir each cup to mix the food coloring with water.
  4. Step 4: Take two colors and pour them into a new cup to see what color they create. Try mixing red with blue, blue with yellow, and yellow with red.
  5. Step 5: Observe the new colors you've created!

Color Mixing Cups:

Observation:

Wow, look at that! When you mix different colors together, you create brand new colors. Red and blue make purple, blue and yellow make green, and red and yellow make orange. It's like a rainbow in a cup! Isn't it amazing how colors can make new friends and create a whole new world of shades? Lest's try friends.

Brand New colors:

Mixing Red and Blue creates purple.

Mixing Blue and Yellow creates green.

Mixing Red and Yellow creates orange.
